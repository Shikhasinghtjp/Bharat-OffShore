Freelance Inspection Services

JAKA Solutions diverse range of inspection services can provide trusted support for your products, projects and processes.

We are committed to high quality Freelance Inspection service, and brings our on-site inspection team to your facility. We are very proud of our team of project managers and inspection personnel who are well guided by professionals with more than 5 years of experience in the industry.

  • 01. Material Verification
    Checking raw materials against project specifications before use.
  • 02. Dimensional Inspection
    Ensuring fabrication dimensions and tolerances are within standard limits.
  • 03. Welding & NDT Review
    Monitoring welding quality, NDT reports and safety compliance.
  • 04. Final Certification
    Issuing detailed inspection reports with approval for dispatch.
