Advanced NDT Testing

Ensuring safety, precision, and reliability through cutting-edge Non-Destructive Testing techniques.

Ultrasonic Testing (UT)

Detects internal flaws and ensures material integrity with high accuracy.

Radiographic Testing (RT)

Uses X-rays & Gamma rays to reveal hidden internal discontinuities.

Magnetic Particle Testing (MPI)

Identifies surface & near-surface defects using magnetic fields.

Dye Penetrant Testing (DPT)

Simple & effective method for detecting cracks and leaks.

Eddy Current Testing (ECT)

Highly sensitive method for detecting surface cracks in conductive materials.

Visual Inspection (VT)

Direct examination to identify surface flaws, weld defects & alignment.
